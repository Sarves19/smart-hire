# Smart Hire

## Project Overview

Smart Hire is a digital marketplace platform designed to connect customers with skilled service providers such as plumbers, electricians, tutors, cleaners, technicians, and other professionals. The platform allows customers to search, book, and review service providers while enabling providers to promote and manage their services.

## Objectives

* Connect customers with trusted service providers
* Simplify service discovery and booking
* Provide ratings and reviews for service quality
* Integrate AI-powered service recommendations
